Gift For Boys

PHP & MYSQL

Gift for Boys is a website focusing on gift ideas for boys. In our survey, more than 50% of boys are not happy with their gifts and they keep receiving similar gifts. Gift for boys is the one to save boys!!!
Our database has recorded the "dream gifts" from boys with different age and culture. We are sure that you can get a gift for your favorite boy here!

ERI was my first attempt to a huge 3D game. The whole game was done by a group of six people. The player controls the character to navigate around the map and to collect different resources. The main goal of the game is to beat the Boss and flee away.

Find the Gap

Java

This is the course project for CPSC 210, a software development course. The whole project is done myself. The user can find nearby subway stations on a map interface. The app also allows users to find nearest station, to get the timetable of certain lines, etc.

Kill a Goblin is a personal project done by a group of 10 people. We started everything from the scratch.
